About Elegant Waterfront Resort

Located in picturesque Talegoan, close to Pune, Elegant Resort’s expansive property is nothing short of a paradise. Experience the true meaning of opulence on a budget when you visit our facility dotted with some of the best immersive stay ideas that can elevate your current standards of serenity.

The pure climate at a height of 2,200 feet above sea level speaks of lush greenery and lakes all around you. You will be delighted at the sight of water bodies engulfing you as you stay in one of the best resorts just 2 hours away from Mumbai.

The Location

The property encompasses cottages with a waterfront view and a beautiful lawn area with a waterbody path. There are villas, suite villas, lawnside cottages, waterside cottages, and suite cottages, all of which consist of a sit-out area where you can enjoy your peace and quiet with a book, a beverage, or chat away with friends and family. While the villas boast of an outdoor pool, the Suite Villa has a pool indoors and comes with a shiny European décor. What’s most enchanting is the Suite Cottage, a hut-like structure with a pool immediately as you enter and a new circular bed!

The spaciousness of the entire hut is amazingly convivial. If you are looking for a unique experience, book the stilt house, which is located at a height and gives you the feeling of living in a tree house, and that too, on a budget.

Our destination resort is extremely proximal to Pune and Mumbai, allowing you to hop on a train or bus to get to us. The resort offers a range of activities, including dining, indoor games, signature experiences, and lots more. It is the scale of the resort that makes it one of the most preferred destinations for weddings, corporate meetings, private parties, ceremonies, and other events.

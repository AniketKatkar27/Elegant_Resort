Wedding & Socials

An expansive resort with multiple indoor & outdoor venues for lavish destination weddings, corporate events, or intimate occasions. We offer a variety of options to cater to the preferences of our guests.


Our dedicated event planning team works closely with clients to curate bespoke experiences, from personalized menus crafted by our renowned chefs to seamless coordination of every detail. Whether it's a dream wedding, a corporate retreat, or a special celebration, our resort promises unparalleled hospitality and unmatched experiences. Beyond events, guests can indulge in a myriad of resort amenities, including rejuvenating spa treatments, exciting water sports, and leisurely activities for all ages. Our culinary offerings extend beyond fine dining to casual eateries and lively bars, ensuring every palate is satisfied. With a commitment to excellence and a passion for creating unforgettable moments, our resort is a destination where dreams come to life and memories are made to last a lifetime.
